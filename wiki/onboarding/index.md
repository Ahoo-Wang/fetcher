# Onboarding Guides

Welcome to the Fetcher onboarding documentation. Choose the guide that matches your role and experience level.

## Available Guides

| Guide | Audience | Focus |
|-------|----------|-------|
| [Contributor Guide](./contributor) | New contributors with JS/TS proficiency | Language foundations, architecture, getting productive |
| [Staff Engineer Guide](./staff-engineer) | Staff and principal engineers | Architectural deep dive, design tradeoffs, decision log |
| [Executive Guide](./executive) | VP/Director-level engineering leaders | Capability map, risk assessment, technology investment |
| [Product Manager Guide](./product-manager) | PMs and non-engineering stakeholders | Feature map, user journeys, competitive landscape |

## How to Use These Guides

- **New to the project?** Start with the [Contributor Guide](./contributor) for a progressive learning path from language basics to architectural understanding.
- **Need architectural context?** The [Staff Engineer Guide](./staff-engineer) provides dense, opinionated analysis of Fetcher's design decisions and tradeoffs.
- **Evaluating Fetcher for your organization?** The [Executive Guide](./executive) covers capabilities, risks, and investment thesis without code-level details.
- **Understanding the product?** The [Product Manager Guide](./product-manager) explains Fetcher's capabilities and market position in non-technical terms.
